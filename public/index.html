Women in Bitcoin

A global community of
women building on Bitcoin

Learn, connect, and grow with women from around the world who are passionate about Bitcoin — from first steps to deep dives.

Learn More
Les Femmes Orange is committed to building an inclusive and supportive community of women all over the world. Our events and meetups are designed to not only teach the practical aspects of Bitcoin, but also to create a welcoming atmosphere where learning, discussing and growing together is a priority.

What we stand for

Built on three pillars

Everything we do flows from a commitment to education, inclusion, and genuine community.

Bitcoin Education

From self-custody to Lightning to mining, we cover the practical skills that matter — taught by and for women.

Inclusive Community

Every background, every level of knowledge — you belong here. We meet people where they are and grow together.

Global Meetups

We gather in cities around the world — in person and online. Wherever you are, there's a seat at the table for you.
