Generating your key pair…

Your key is your identity

Your Nostr key pair is generated entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. You'll receive a public key (npub) to share, and a secret key (nsec) to keep private — it's the only way to access your account.

Public key (npub)

Safe to share — this is your Nostr identity

Secret key (nsec)

Your private key — treat it like a password

Never share your nsec. Anyone who has it can take over your identity. Don't paste it into sites you don't trust.

You'll need your nsec every time you sign in. Save it before continuing.
