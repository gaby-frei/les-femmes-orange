## Voice
Tone, register, personality. Who the product sounds like.

## Language rules
- No AI-generated filler ("I'd be happy to help," "Great question," "Let's dive in").
- No marketing superlatives without evidence.
- No jargon without definition.
- Active voice over passive.
- Short sentences over compound.
- Specific over vague ("3 curators rated this 5 stars," not "highly rated").

## UI copy patterns
- **Button labels:** verb + noun ("Add to shelf," not "Submit").
- **Empty states:** describe what will appear here and how to start.
- **Error messages:** what went wrong and what to do about it.
- **Confirmation messages:** confirm the action, not the click ("Book added to your shelf," not "Submitted successfully").
